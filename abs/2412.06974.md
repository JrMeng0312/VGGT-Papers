### MV-DUSt3R+: Single-Stage Scene Reconstruction from Sparse Views In 2 Seconds

We address limitations in existing multi-view reconstruction methods by proposing a feed-forward network that processes multiple views simultaneously rather than in pairs. Key innovations include multi-view decoder blocks for cross-view information exchange and cross-reference-view blocks to reduce sensitivity to reference view selection. The method also incorporates Gaussian splatting capabilities for novel view synthesis. Our approach improves significantly upon prior art in multi-view stereo reconstruction, pose estimation, and novel view synthesis, while reducing computational overhead compared to methods requiring global optimization steps.

### VGGT: Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer

We introduce VGGT, a feed-forward neural network that directly infers all key 3D attributes of a scene, including camera parameters, point maps, depth maps, and 3D point tracks, from one, a few, or hundreds of its views. VGGT is a large feed-forward transformer with minimal 3D-inductive biases trained on a large amount of 3D-annotated data. It accepts up to hundreds of images and predicts cameras, point maps, depth maps, and point tracks for all images at once in less than a second. The network achieves state-of-the-art results in multiple 3D tasks, including camera parameter estimation, multi-view depth estimation, dense point cloud reconstruction, and 3D point tracking. We additionally demonstrate that using VGGT as a feature backbone enhances downstream applications like non-rigid point tracking and feed-forward novel view synthesis.

我们介绍了 VGGT，一种前馈神经网络，能够从一张、几张或数百张视图中直接推断场景的所有关键三维属性，包括相机参数、点图、深度图和三维点轨迹。VGGT 是一个具有最小三维归纳偏置的大型前馈 Transformer，在大量三维标注数据上训练。它可以接受多达数百张图像，并在不到一秒内同时预测所有图像的相机参数、点图、深度图和点轨迹。该网络在多个三维任务中取得了最先进的结果，包括相机参数估计、多视图深度估计、稠密点云重建和三维点跟踪。我们还展示了将 VGGT 用作特征骨干网络可以增强非刚性点跟踪和前馈新视图合成等下游应用。

### PAGE-4D: Disentangled Pose and Geometry Estimation for VGGT-4D Perception

We present a feedforward model extending Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer capabilities to dynamic scenes, enabling camera pose estimation, depth prediction, and point cloud reconstruction without post-processing. We address a key challenge where pose estimation and geometry reconstruction have conflicting requirements — suppressing versus modeling dynamic regions respectively. Our solution introduces a dynamics-aware aggregator that disentangles static and dynamic information by predicting a dynamics-aware mask. Testing demonstrates superior performance compared to the original VGGT across multiple tasks including monocular and video depth estimation alongside dense point map reconstruction in dynamic scenarios.

我们提出了一个前馈模型，将视觉几何锚定 Transformer 的能力扩展到动态场景，实现无需后处理的相机位姿估计、深度预测和点云重建。我们解决了位姿估计和几何重建之间需求冲突的关键挑战——分别需要抑制和建模动态区域。我们的解决方案引入了一个动态感知聚合器，通过预测动态感知掩码来解耦静态和动态信息。测试表明，在包括单目和视频深度估计以及动态场景中稠密点图重建在内的多个任务上，性能优于原始 VGGT。

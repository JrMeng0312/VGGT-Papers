### VGGT-SLAM 2.0: Real-time Dense Feed-forward Scene Reconstruction

We present an improved RGB feed-forward SLAM system that substantially improves upon VGGT-SLAM for incrementally aligning submaps created from VGGT. Key enhancements include addressing drift issues and planar degeneracy through a redesigned factor graph while managing reconstruction ambiguity from unknown camera intrinsics. We identified an attention layer in VGGT suitable for image retrieval verification without additional training, enabling better false positive rejection and increased loop closures. Evaluation on the TUM dataset shows about 23 percent less pose error than VGGT-SLAM. The system demonstrates real-time capability on a Jetson Thor robot.

我们提出了一个改进的 RGB 前馈 SLAM 系统，显著改善了 VGGT-SLAM 在增量对齐由 VGGT 创建的子图方面的表现。关键改进包括通过重新设计的因子图解决漂移问题和平面退化，同时管理因未知相机内参导致的重建模糊性。我们发现了 VGGT 中一个适合图像检索验证的注意力层，无需额外训练即可实现更好的假阳性拒绝和更多闭环。在 TUM 数据集上的评估显示位姿误差比 VGGT-SLAM 减少约 23%。该系统在 Jetson Thor 机器人上展示了实时能力。

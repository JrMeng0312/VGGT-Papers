### Grounding Image Matching in 3D with MASt3R

We propose treating image matching as a 3D task rather than a 2D problem. Building on DUSt3R, we augment the network with a dense local features head and matching loss. To address computational complexity, we introduce a fast reciprocal matching scheme that not only accelerates matching by orders of magnitude, but also comes with theoretical guarantees. Our method, MASt3R, outperforms existing approaches significantly, achieving 30% absolute improvement in VCRE AUC on the extremely challenging Map-free localization dataset, while maintaining robustness across extreme viewpoint variations.

我们提出将图像匹配视为三维任务而非二维问题。在 DUSt3R 的基础上，我们为网络增加了稠密局部特征头和匹配损失。为解决计算复杂性，我们引入了一种快速互惠匹配方案，不仅将匹配加速了数个数量级，而且具有理论保证。我们的方法 MASt3R 显著优于现有方法，在极具挑战性的无地图定位数据集上实现了 VCRE AUC 30% 的绝对提升，同时在极端视角变化下保持了鲁棒性。

# MR.ScaleMaster: Scale-Consistent Collaborative Mapping from Crowd-Sourced Monocular Videos

**arXiv:** [2604.11372](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11372)

## Abstract

Crowd-sourced cooperative mapping from monocular cameras promises scalable 3D reconstruction without specialized sensors, yet remains hindered by two scale-specific failure modes: abrupt scale collapse from false-positive loop closures in repetitive environments, and gradual scale drift over long trajectories and per-robot scale ambiguity that prevent direct multi-session fusion. MR.ScaleMaster is a cooperative mapping system for crowd-sourced monocular videos that addresses both failure modes. It introduces three key mechanisms: First, a Scale Collapse Alarm rejects spurious loop closures before they corrupt the pose graph. Second, a Sim(3) anchor node formulation generalizes the classical SE(3) framework to explicitly estimate per-session scale, resolving per-robot scale ambiguity and enforcing global scale consistency. Third, a modular, open-source, plug-and-play interface enables any monocular reconstruction model to integrate without backend modification. On KITTI sequences with up to 15 agents, the Sim(3) formulation achieves a 7.2x ATE reduction over the SE(3) baseline, and the alarm rejects all false-positive loops while preserving every valid constraint. Heterogeneous multi-robot dense mapping fusing MASt3R-SLAM, pi3, and VGGT-SLAM 2.0 within a single unified map is demonstrated.

## 摘要

基于单目相机的众包协作式建图，有望在无需专用传感器的前提下实现可扩展的 3D 重建，但长期受制于两种尺度相关的失效模式：重复场景中由假阳性闭环（false-positive loop closures）引发的**突发性尺度崩塌（scale collapse）**，以及长轨迹下的**渐进式尺度漂移**和每个机器人自身的尺度歧义，这些因素都阻碍了多会话（multi-session）地图的直接融合。MR.ScaleMaster 是一个针对众包单目视频的协作建图系统，同时解决了上述两类问题。其核心机制有三：第一，**Scale Collapse Alarm（尺度崩塌告警）**在假阳性闭环污染位姿图之前将其剔除；第二，**Sim(3) 锚节点（anchor node）**建模方式将经典 SE(3) 框架推广至显式估计每个会话的尺度，从而解决单机尺度歧义并保证全局尺度一致性；第三，模块化、开源、即插即用的接口允许任意单目重建模型集成进来而无需修改后端。在最多 15 个代理的 KITTI 序列上，Sim(3) 版本相比 SE(3) 基线将 ATE 降低 7.2 倍，告警机制在保留所有有效约束的同时拒绝了全部假阳性闭环。实验还展示了将 MASt3R-SLAM、pi3 和 VGGT-SLAM 2.0 融合在同一统一地图中的异构多机器人稠密建图能力。

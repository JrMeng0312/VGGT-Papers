### DriveVGGT: Calibration-Constrained Visual Geometry Transformers for Multi-Camera Autonomous Driving

We address limitations in applying Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer (VGGT) to autonomous driving by identifying three domain-specific gaps. We propose DriveVGGT to handle sparse spatial overlap among multi-view cameras through a Temporal Video Attention module, leverage calibration information via a Multi-camera Consistency Attention module for absolute scale reconstruction, and utilize rigid camera pose relationships through reformulated decoding with separate pose and ego-motion heads. Experimental results demonstrate 49.3% inference time reduction and improved depth/pose estimation compared to vanilla VGGT in extended sequence scenarios.

我们通过识别三个特定领域差距来解决将视觉几何锚定 Transformer（VGGT）应用于自动驾驶的局限性。我们提出 DriveVGGT，通过时间视频注意力模块处理多视图相机之间的稀疏空间重叠，通过多相机一致性注意力模块利用标定信息实现绝对尺度重建，并通过重新设计的解码（包含独立的位姿和自运动头）利用刚性相机位姿关系。实验结果表明，在扩展序列场景中，与原始 VGGT 相比，推理时间减少 49.3%，深度/位姿估计有所改善。

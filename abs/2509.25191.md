### VGGT-X: When VGGT Meets Dense Novel View Synthesis

We study the problem of applying 3D Foundation Models (3DFMs) to dense Novel View Synthesis (NVS). Despite significant progress in Novel View Synthesis powered by NeRF and 3DGS, current approaches remain reliant on accurate 3D attributes acquired from Structure-from-Motion (SfM), which is often slow and fragile in low-texture or low-overlap captures. Recent 3DFMs showcase orders of magnitude speedup over the traditional pipeline and great potential for online NVS. But most of the validation and conclusions are confined to sparse-view settings. Our study reveals that naively scaling 3DFMs to dense views encounters two fundamental barriers: dramatically increasing VRAM burden and imperfect outputs that degrade initialization-sensitive 3D training. To address these barriers, we introduce VGGT-X, incorporating a memory-efficient VGGT implementation that scales to 1,000+ images, an adaptive global alignment for VGGT output enhancement, and robust 3DGS training practices. Extensive experiments show that these measures substantially close the fidelity gap with COLMAP-initialized pipelines, achieving state-of-the-art results in dense COLMAP-free NVS and pose estimation.

我们研究将 3D 基础模型（3DFMs）应用于稠密新视角合成（NVS）的问题。尽管 NeRF 和 3DGS 驱动的新视角合成已取得显著进展，但当前方法仍依赖于从运动恢复结构（SfM）获取的精确 3D 属性，而 SfM 在低纹理或低重叠捕获场景中往往缓慢且脆弱。近期的 3DFMs 展示了比传统流水线快数个数量级的速度提升，以及在线 NVS 的巨大潜力。但大多数验证和结论仅限于稀疏视角设定。我们的研究揭示，简单地将 3DFMs 扩展到稠密视角会遇到两个根本障碍：显存需求急剧增加，以及不完美的输出会降低对初始化敏感的 3D 训练质量。为解决这些障碍，我们引入 VGGT-X，包含一个可扩展到 1000+ 张图像的内存高效 VGGT 实现、自适应全局对齐用于增强 VGGT 输出，以及鲁棒的 3DGS 训练策略。大量实验表明，这些措施大幅缩小了与 COLMAP 初始化流水线的保真度差距，在无 COLMAP 的稠密 NVS 和位姿估计中达到了最先进水平。

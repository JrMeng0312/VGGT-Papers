### DUSt3R: Geometric 3D Vision Made Easy

We present a novel approach to multi-view stereo reconstruction that eliminates the need for prior camera calibration information. Rather than requiring intrinsic and extrinsic camera parameters, our method reformulates the pairwise reconstruction task as pointmap regression, accommodating both monocular and binocular scenarios. For multi-image sequences, we introduce a global alignment strategy to unify pointmaps in a shared reference frame. The architecture leverages standard Transformer components with pretrained models. The technique simultaneously produces 3D scene models, depth maps, pixel correspondences, and camera pose estimates, achieving state-of-the-art results across multiple 3D vision tasks.

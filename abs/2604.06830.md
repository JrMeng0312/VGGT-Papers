### VGGT-SLAM++

We present a complete visual SLAM system that leverages the geometry-rich outputs of the Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer (VGGT). The system combines visual odometry using transformer technology with Sim(3) solutions, a Digital Elevation Map-based graph module, and a back-end for large-scale mapping with controlled memory use. A key advancement involves restoring high-cadence local bundle adjustment (LBA) through a spatially corrective back-end. The method constructs dense planar-canonical Digital Elevation Maps for each submap, partitions them into patches, and computes DINOv2 embeddings for graph integration. Visual Place Recognition within a covisibility window enables frequent local optimization, substantially reducing short-term drift.

我们提出了一个完整的视觉 SLAM 系统，利用了视觉几何锚定 Transformer（VGGT）的丰富几何输出。该系统结合了使用 Transformer 技术的视觉里程计与 Sim(3) 解、基于数字高程图的图模块以及用于大规模建图且内存可控的后端。关键进步在于通过空间校正后端恢复了高频次局部光束平差（LBA）。该方法为每个子图构建稠密的平面规范数字高程图，将其分割为块，并计算 DINOv2 嵌入用于图集成。共视窗口内的视觉位置识别实现了频繁的局部优化，显著减少了短期漂移。

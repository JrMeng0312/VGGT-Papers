### AMB3R: Accurate Feed-forward Metric-scale 3D Reconstruction with Backend

We introduce AMB3R, a multi-view feed-forward model for dense 3D scene reconstruction at metric scale. The system leverages a sparse volumetric representation as its backend to enable geometric reasoning while maintaining spatial efficiency. While trained specifically for multi-view reconstruction, the model demonstrates flexibility for uncalibrated visual odometry and large-scale structure from motion tasks without the need for task-specific fine-tuning or test-time optimization. We achieve state-of-the-art results in camera pose, depth, and metric-scale estimation, outperforming earlier pointmap-based approaches and rivaling optimization-based SLAM and SfM methods on standard benchmarks.

我们介绍了 AMB3R，一种用于度量尺度稠密三维场景重建的多视图前馈模型。该系统利用稀疏体素表示作为后端，在保持空间效率的同时实现几何推理。虽然专门为多视图重建训练，但该模型展示了对未标定视觉里程计和大规模运动恢复结构任务的灵活性，无需任务特定的微调或测试时优化。我们在相机位姿、深度和度量尺度估计方面取得了最先进的结果，优于早期基于点图的方法，并在标准基准上可与基于优化的 SLAM 和 SfM 方法相媲美。

### VGGT-SLAM: Dense RGB SLAM Optimized on the SL(4) Manifold

We present a dense RGB SLAM system constructed by incrementally and globally aligning submaps created from the feed-forward scene reconstruction approach VGGT using only uncalibrated monocular cameras. We demonstrate that conventional similarity transform approaches are insufficient for uncalibrated scenarios. We revisit the concept of reconstruction ambiguity, noting that uncalibrated cameras can only reconstruct scenes up to a 15-degrees-of-freedom projective transformation of the true geometry. To address this, we optimize across the SL(4) manifold to estimate 15-DOF homography transforms between sequential submaps while handling loop closure constraints.

我们提出了一个稠密 RGB SLAM 系统，通过仅使用未标定的单目相机，从前馈场景重建方法 VGGT 创建的子图进行增量和全局对齐构建。我们证明了传统的相似变换方法对于未标定场景是不够的。我们重新审视了重建模糊性的概念，指出未标定相机只能将场景重建到真实几何的 15 自由度射影变换。为此，我们在 SL(4) 流形上优化，以估计连续子图之间的 15 自由度单应变换，同时处理闭环约束。

### MoGe: Unlocking Accurate Monocular Geometry Estimation for Open-Domain Images with Optimal Training Supervision

We present MoGe, a system for extracting three-dimensional geometry information from single images of real-world scenes. Our approach uses an affine-invariant representation to predict point cloud data without requiring absolute scale information, which reduces training ambiguity. We introduce novel supervision techniques including a robust, optimal, and efficient point cloud alignment solver and multi-scale local geometry constraints. Testing across multiple datasets demonstrates substantial improvements over existing techniques for depth estimation, three-dimensional point cloud prediction, and field-of-view determination.

我们提出了 MoGe，一个从真实世界场景的单张图像中提取三维几何信息的系统。我们的方法使用仿射不变表示来预测点云数据，无需绝对尺度信息，从而减少了训练模糊性。我们引入了新颖的监督技术，包括一个鲁棒、最优且高效的点云对齐求解器和多尺度局部几何约束。在多个数据集上的测试表明，在深度估计、三维点云预测和视场角确定方面，比现有技术有显著改进。

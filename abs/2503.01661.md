### MUSt3R: Multi-view Network for Stereo 3D Reconstruction

DUSt3R introduced a novel paradigm in geometric computer vision by proposing a model that can provide dense and unconstrained Stereo 3D Reconstruction of arbitrary image collections with no prior information about camera calibration nor viewpoint poses. We extend this work by proposing MUSt3R, which processes multiple views simultaneously rather than image pairs. Key innovations include a symmetric architecture that predicts 3D structure in a common coordinate frame and a multi-layer memory mechanism. The approach reduces computational complexity and enables processing of large image collections at high frame rates, demonstrating effectiveness in both offline and online reconstruction tasks including visual odometry, pose estimation, and depth prediction.

DUSt3R 通过提出一个能够在没有相机标定和视点位姿先验信息的情况下，对任意图像集合提供稠密且无约束的立体三维重建的模型，在几何计算机视觉中引入了新范式。我们通过提出 MUSt3R 扩展了这项工作，它同时处理多个视图而非图像对。关键创新包括在公共坐标系中预测三维结构的对称架构和多层记忆机制。该方法降低了计算复杂度，能够以高帧率处理大型图像集合，在离线和在线重建任务（包括视觉里程计、位姿估计和深度预测）中展示了有效性。

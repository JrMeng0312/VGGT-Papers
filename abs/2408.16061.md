### Spann3R: 3D Reconstruction with Spatial Memory

We present Spann3R, a novel method for dense 3D reconstruction from image collections. Built on the DUSt3R framework, our transformer-based approach directly regresses pointmaps from images without requiring prior scene or camera knowledge. The key advancement distinguishing Spann3R from DUSt3R is its ability to generate per-image pointmaps in a global coordinate system, rather than local frames. This eliminates the need for subsequent optimization-based alignment. The technique maintains an external spatial memory that learns to keep track of all previous relevant 3D information and queries this memory to predict 3D structure in global coordinates. Leveraging DUSt3R's pre-trained weights and additional fine-tuning, Spann3R demonstrates competitive performance across various unseen datasets with strong generalization.

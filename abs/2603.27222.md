### HD-VGGT: High-Resolution Visual Geometry Transformer

High-resolution imagery is essential for accurate 3D reconstruction, as many geometric details only emerge at fine spatial scales. However, existing feed-forward approaches struggle with computational costs when processing high-resolution inputs across multiple views. HD-VGGT employs a dual-branch design: a low-resolution pathway generates coarse geometry with global consistency, while a high-resolution branch refines details through learned feature upsampling. To combat unreliable features in visually ambiguous regions, we introduce a Feature Modulation technique that suppresses questionable features early in processing. This approach achieves state-of-the-art reconstruction quality while avoiding full-resolution transformer computational overhead.

高分辨率图像对于精确的三维重建至关重要，因为许多几何细节仅在精细的空间尺度下才能呈现。然而，现有的前馈方法在处理多视图高分辨率输入时面临计算成本问题。HD-VGGT 采用双分支设计：低分辨率通路生成具有全局一致性的粗糙几何，而高分辨率分支通过学习的特征上采样细化细节。为了应对视觉模糊区域中不可靠的特征，我们引入了特征调制技术，在处理早期抑制可疑特征。该方法在避免全分辨率 Transformer 计算开销的同时实现了最先进的重建质量。

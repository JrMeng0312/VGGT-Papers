### Review of Feed-forward 3D Reconstruction: From DUSt3R to VGGT

This review examines 3D reconstruction methods that employ a direct inference approach. Deep learning has catalyzed a paradigm shift in 3D reconstruction, with models like DUSt3R enabling single-pass estimation of camera positions and dense geometry from unrestricted image sets. Key technical areas covered include Transformer-based correspondence analysis, joint pose and geometry prediction, and scaling strategies for multi-view processing. We compare this emerging approach against both traditional pipelines (Structure from Motion and Multi-View Stereo) and earlier learning-based techniques like MVSNet. The paper also addresses datasets, evaluation approaches, and discusses implementation challenges including model precision, scalability, and dynamic scene handling.

本综述审视了采用直接推理方法的三维重建方法。深度学习催化了三维重建的范式转变，DUSt3R 等模型能够从不受约束的图像集合中单次推断相机位置和稠密几何。涵盖的关键技术领域包括基于 Transformer 的对应分析、联合位姿和几何预测以及多视图处理的扩展策略。我们将这一新兴方法与传统流水线（运动恢复结构和多视图立体）以及早期基于学习的技术（如 MVSNet）进行了比较。论文还讨论了数据集、评估方法，以及包括模型精度、可扩展性和动态场景处理在内的实现挑战。

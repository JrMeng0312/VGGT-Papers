### SwiftVGGT: A Scalable Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer for Large-Scale Scenes

We introduce a training-free approach that significantly reduces inference time while preserving high-quality dense 3D reconstruction in large-scale scenes. Our method performs loop closure without relying on external Visual Place Recognition models, enabling accurate reconstruction across kilometer-scale areas. We propose a point sampling technique using a single Sim(3)-based Singular Value Decomposition (SVD) step, eliminating the need for iterative optimization. SwiftVGGT achieves state-of-the-art reconstruction quality while requiring only 33% of the inference time of recent VGGT-based large-scale reconstruction approaches.

我们提出了一种免训练方法，在保持高质量稠密三维重建的同时显著减少了大规模场景的推理时间。我们的方法无需依赖外部视觉位置识别模型即可执行闭环检测，实现跨千米级区域的精确重建。我们提出了一种使用单次基于 Sim(3) 的奇异值分解（SVD）步骤的点采样技术，消除了迭代优化的需要。SwiftVGGT 实现了最先进的重建质量，同时仅需近期基于 VGGT 的大规模重建方法 33% 的推理时间。

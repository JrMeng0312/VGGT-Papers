### MASt3R-SfM: a Fully-Integrated Solution for Unconstrained Structure-from-Motion

Structure-from-Motion (SfM) tackles the challenge of jointly recovering camera poses and 3D geometry of a scene given a set of images. Traditional pipelines employ complex minimal solvers prone to error propagation and failure with insufficient image overlap. This work leverages a recent foundation model for 3D vision that produces robust local reconstructions and accurate matches. We introduce a memory-efficient alignment method for these reconstructions in global coordinates. The foundation model also serves as an efficient image retriever without additional overhead, reducing computational complexity from quadratic to linear. The resulting pipeline is simple, scalable, fast and truly unconstrained, i.e. it can handle any collection of images, ordered or not.

运动恢复结构（SfM）解决的是在给定一组图像的情况下联合恢复相机位姿和场景三维几何的挑战。传统流水线采用复杂的最小求解器，容易出现误差传播和图像重叠不足时的失败。本工作利用了一个最近的三维视觉基础模型，能够产生鲁棒的局部重建和精确的匹配。我们引入了一种内存高效的全局坐标对齐方法。该基础模型还可以作为高效的图像检索器而无需额外开销，将计算复杂度从二次降低到线性。最终的流水线简单、可扩展、快速且真正无约束，即它可以处理任何图像集合，无论有序还是无序。
